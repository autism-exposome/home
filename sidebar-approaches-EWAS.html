EWAS


Environment-wide association studies (EWAS) is an analytic approach, similar to Genome-wide association studies, which is to identify the assoociation between exposures and disease/disease related traits. Mutiple exposures without considering interations are assessed simutaneously by EWAS (Patel. 2014).
